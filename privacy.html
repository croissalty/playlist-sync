privacy policy

effective date: june 14, 2026

playlist sync is a personal automation tool that synchronizes playlists between spotify and youtube.

information accessed

the app may access spotify playlist information and youtube playlist information that the user authorizes through their respective authentication systems.

this may include playlist names, playlist contents, track information, video information, and related metadata necessary to compare and synchronize playlists.

how information is used

the information is used solely to compare playlists between spotify and youtube and to add missing songs or videos so that both playlists remain synchronized.

data sharing

playlist sync does not sell, rent, share, or disclose user data to third parties.

data storage

playlist data may be temporarily processed during synchronization operations.

authentication tokens may be stored within the user's self-hosted automation environment in order to perform authorized synchronization tasks.

data deletion

users may revoke access through their spotify account settings, google account settings, or by removing credentials from their automation environment.

contact

questions regarding this privacy policy may be directed to the application owner.
